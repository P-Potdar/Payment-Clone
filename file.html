    A Full-Scale Payment Gateway Platform
    with a Fully Integrated Solutions Suite

    QC Lite

    A one-of-a-kind payment solution for your customers wherein payments can be made without the user leaving your application. QC Lite auto selects the most preferred payment option among saved instruments with PhonePe and also helps generate up to 4% incremental success rates.

    Fast Forward

    A lightning-fast checkout experience that reduces the steps in a user's payment journey to just one. Enabling faster payments, the experience helps you generate incremental success rates upto 3%.

    Dynamic QR

    An intuitive two-step payment experience of scanning a QR code using any UPI app to make the transaction. Generating better conversions by reducing drop-offs and safeguards you from VPA data entry errors.

    Auto-Pay/Recurring

    A one-click subscription solution that is easy to integrate and easier to set up by your customers. It fosters growth for your business by ensuring revenue lock-in and user retention.

    Pay On Delivery

    Seamless and contactless payment solution facilitating digital payments for COD orders at the doorstep of your customers, helping your business to reduce the cash handling costs.

    Tokenization

    Store your tokenized cards exclusively for your customers with your very own PhonePe Card Vault. PhonePe acts as the Technical Service Provider (TSP) for you and provides you with the complete ownership of all tokenized cards in the vault. The solution supports other Payment Gateways and external tokens as well.
